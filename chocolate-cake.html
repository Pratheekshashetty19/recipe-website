Chocolate Cake

Home Desserts Salads

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup milk
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Pour into a greased cake pan.
  4. Bake for 30-35 minutes.
