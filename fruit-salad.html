Fruit Salad

Home Desserts Salads

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped apples
  • 1 cup sliced bananas
  • 1/2 cup grapes
  • 1/2 cup orange segments
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp honey

Instructions

  1. Wash and chop all the fruits.
  2. Combine them in a large bowl.
  3. Add lemon juice and honey, then mix gently.
  4. Serve fresh and chilled!
